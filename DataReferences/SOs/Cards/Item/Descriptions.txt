Ink Cow:
Generates ink over time. The magic of a leyline empowers cows, doubling their ink generation. Suprisingly, this cow does not require any milking.

Rough Ground:
Slows the movement and attacks of enemies walking over it. A kilogram of it actually weighs less than a kilogram of feathers.

Spike Bush:
Deals damage to back to enemies when they attack it. Its spikes are strong enough to penetrate steel.
